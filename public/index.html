Welcome to the Ayush FHIR Microservice Demo

This application demonstrates interoperability between traditional Indian medicine terminologies (NAMASTE) and global standards (ICD-11 FHIR).

Key Features:

  • Search and translate NAMASTE and ICD-11 terms.
  • Ingest NAMASTE CSV data.
  • Generate FHIR CodeSystem and ConceptMap resources.
  • Simulate ABHA-like OAuth and capture audit trails.
